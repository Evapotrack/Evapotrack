Features

Organize Your Grows

Group plants into grows with custom names, pot sizes, and medium types. Track up to 30 grows with 25 plants each.

Log Every Watering

Record water added, runoff collected, temperature, and humidity. Retention and runoff percentages calculated automatically.

Smart Recommendations

Get next watering recommendations based on historical patterns and your target runoff percentage.

Visualize Trends

Track retained water, temperature, and humidity over time with interactive charts and overlay toggles.

Capacity Tracking

Monitor plant saturation levels with capacity percentage tracking against your medium's max retention.

Export Your Data

Download grow data as a formatted text file for your records, with all values in your preferred display units.

Flexible Units

Milliliters, liters, and gallons. Fahrenheit and Celsius. Day and Dark mode. English and Spanish.

iPhone & iPad

Designed for both iPhone and iPad with adaptive layouts. Portrait optimized for quick, one-handed logging.

Your Data Stays Yours

No accounts. No cloud. No tracking. All data is stored locally on your device. Evapotrack makes zero network connections.
