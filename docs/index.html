What Is Evapotrack?

Evapotrack is a plant watering tracker for iPhone and iPad. It measures water input and runoff to calculate how much water your plants actually retain. Built for drain-to-waste growers running coco, soil, or any medium where tracking runoff matters. Log each watering, monitor retention and capacity over time, and get data-driven recommendations for your next watering — all offline, all stored locally on your device.
