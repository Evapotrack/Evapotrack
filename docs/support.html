Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. Evapotrack does not use cloud storage or send data to any server.

How do I export my data?

Open a grow, tap the gear icon to open Settings, then use the Download Data button to export your grow data as a text file.

How do I delete a grow or plant?

Tap the circle next to the item to select it, then tap the trash icon. A confirmation will appear before anything is deleted.

Will I lose my data if I delete the app?

Yes. Since all data is stored locally on your device, uninstalling the app will permanently remove all grows, plants, and watering logs.

What devices are supported?

Evapotrack supports iPhone and iPad running iOS 17 or later.
